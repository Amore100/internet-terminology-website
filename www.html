World Wide Web (WWW)

The World Wide Web (WWW) is essentially the universe of network-accessible information, an embodiment of human knowledge. To make this clear for your website's index, it helps to break it down into a simple, neat explanation that avoids the "tech-jargon" trap.

🌐 What is the World Wide Web? While people often use "The Internet" and "The Web" interchangeably, they aren't the same thing.

Think of the Internet as the hardware (the roads and pipes) and the World Wide Web as the traffic (the cars and houses) that sits on top of it.
